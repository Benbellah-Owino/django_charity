DIRECT LINK

Direct Link Charity organization is an NGO company that is located in kahawa Sukari, kiambu town. It focuses on helping the poor in all areas of the country in every way they can manage. The organization is made up of the CEO, 3 team leaders and volunteers. They usually set up charity events in different parts of the country for fundraisings and also to come across the needy who need assistance. Most times the funds received are first gathered then distributed to people they had identified to help. Donors who make donations online currently have only two payment methods which is the company’s mpesa till number which they acquire after contacting the organization and physically handing out cash which are later deposited in the organization’s account. The organization’s data is all stored in files by one of the team leader appointed as the accountant
